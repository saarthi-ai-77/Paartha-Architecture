- **ID:** KFC-DEC-001
- **Decision:** The research bottleneck must shift from defining "knowledge" to discovering fundamental "computational primitives".
- **Rationale:** The council realized that any structural definition of knowledge relies on concepts (like Distinction, Constraint, or Relation) whose irreducibility has not been proven. Identifying true computational primitives must precede defining knowledge.
- **Supporting Evidence:** The Reductionist successfully collapsed six proposed primitives into essentially Distinction and Constraint. The Scientific Reviewer subsequently demonstrated that the primitive status of Constraint itself remains unproven.
- **Date:** 2026-07-17
- **Status:** Active


- **ID:** KFC-DEC-002

- **Rationale:** These concepts are structural consequences of deeper distinctions rather than foundational entities themselves.
- **Supporting Evidence:** The Reductionist successfully collapsed System, Environment, and Coupling into Distinction. The Scientific Reviewer and Research Theorist confirmed that Constraint is derived and belongs downstream.

- **Status:** Active

- **ID:** KFC-DEC-003
- **Decision:** Sprint 3 will focus on developing a formal validation methodology for computational primitives rather than proposing new candidates.
- **Rationale:** Without a rigorous procedure for proving irreducibility and providing falsifiability, any claim of primitiveness remains philosophical rather than scientific.
- **Supporting Evidence:** The Scientific Reviewer's primary critique in Sprint 2 was the lack of a falsification procedure and formal proof mechanism for the proposed primitives.
- **Date:** 2026-07-17
- **Status:** Active


- **ID:** KFC-DEC-004
- **Decision:** Foundational concepts (primitives) are provisionally accepted through survival under criticism, not permanently proven.
- **Rationale:** Proving primitiveness requires universal quantification over all possible theories and derivations, which is impossible. Disproving it requires only one counterexample. Therefore, absolute proof is unattainable.
- **Supporting Evidence:** Full council consensus during Sprint 3. The falsification asymmetry aligns with Popperian methodology and avoids impossible epistemic standards.
- **Date:** 2026-07-17
- **Status:** Active

- **ID:** KFC-DEC-005

The remaining protocol stages (Registration, Surveillance, etc.) serve as vital governance and process mechanisms rather than core scientific principles.
- **Supporting Evidence:** Synthesis of the Reductionist's simplification of the PRIMITIVE protocol and the CTO's distinction between scientific logic and research process.
- **Date:** 2026-07-17
- **Status:** Active
