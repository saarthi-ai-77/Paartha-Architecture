# Research Goal

To build and empirically validate a modular architecture — one component at a time, each tested against an honest baseline before being trusted — capable of dynamically composing representations, learning objectives, and inference methods tailored to distinct kinds of knowledge, rather than forcing all knowledge through one uniform computational process. Classification of knowledge is no longer a required precondition for this work: individual mechanisms (memory allocation, rule/family selection) are validated directly through experiment, and any taxonomy this program eventually needs will be derived from what those experiments actually show, not proposed in advance of them.
