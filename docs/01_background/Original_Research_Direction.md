# Original Research Direction

## The Initial Objective

The project originally aimed to create a **Web Design Foundation Model**. The goal was to overcome the limitations of LLMs in producing structured, aesthetically coherent, and spatially aware designs.

## Why it Evolved

During Council reviews and architectural exploration, it became clear that the difficulties in design generation were not isolated to the domain of web design. They were symptoms of a much larger flaw in current AI paradigms: applying a uniform computational process (like next-token prediction) to all cognitive tasks.

## Why it was Not Abandoned

The design domain was never abandoned; it was correctly reclassified. The original design model vision became the primary **evaluation domain** and the source of crucial evidence that led to the current research into generalized adaptive computation and knowledge foundations.
