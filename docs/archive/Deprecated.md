## Superseded: `docs/02_vision/Success_Criteria.md` (previously "Status: Frozen")

*Deprecated 2026-07-18. Superseded because it treated the quarantined `council/main` RP-001 taxonomy (the "C2⊗C3 interactive manifold" framing) as settled, and its "Reproducibility" criterion described the council methodology as the sole path from first principles to architectural decisions — both no longer accurate after DEC-005.*

> *   **Scientific Clarity:** The successful derivation of a defensible, minimal taxonomy of knowledge based on computational structure rather than academic discipline.
> *   **Architectural Validation:** The theoretical validation that the proposed adaptive architecture resolves the sample complexity and generalization limitations seen in uniform architectures when applied to distinct knowledge classes (e.g., pure compositional vs. pure interactive tasks).
> *   **Domain Evaluation:** The eventual empirical demonstration that the architecture can autonomously navigate the C2⊗C3 interactive manifold of design cognition.
> *   **Reproducibility:** A clear, traceable, and historically preserved path from first principles to architectural decisions via the Council methodology.
